About Implicit

Implicit Learning Centre (ILC) is one of the most well known shadow institutions for HSC academic students in Dania, Dhaka. It's been a journey of more than 10 years. Since 2008 till now, a good number of our students had been able to grab their seats in many public universities including DU, Jnu, JU and so on.

Why Choose ILC

In academic lessons we will support from the very begining even it could be play group students to job preperations of freshly baked graduates.

ILC always tries to make segments of the whole 1st and 2nd year syllabus and put them into a frame just to follow them effectively.

Apart from senior teachers, ILC has always been interested to provide services by expert and experienced students of renowned public universities.

In non academic section you can get some skill development lessons of every aspects of life so far we can go.

It's been a tradition of ILC to keep all the batches as small as possible just to ensure proper care to all.

We believe that there is no boundary of teaching and learning.
